About PNV Lifesciences

⦁ Established in 2015, PNV Lifesciences is an innovation-driven¸ people-centric & client-focused organization offering a broad and integrated portfolio of products & services to the global pharmaceutical industry.
⦁ We specialize in supplies of API, RLDs and also in Business Development for selected markets.
⦁ At PNV Lifesciences, we understand the challenges faced by Pharmaceutical & Lifesciences industry. It is becoming increasingly evident that the Global life sciences sector is operating in an era of significant transformation.
⦁ A dynamically changing clinical, regulatory, and business landscape is requiring that pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical technology companies adapt traditional research and development (R&D), pricing, supply chain, and commercial models to Support value-based payments, Curtail cost, Maintain Regulatory Compliance & Focus on Emerging Market.
⦁ Our mission at PNV Lifesciences is to provide Quality Solutions and Services efficiently through collaboration so that our partners and customer can achieve their Business Goals. We strive for long term relationship with our clients through our focus on providing cost effective solutions, Customer Satisfaction, commitment to Quality and exceeding Customer Expectations. Most of our relationships begins with a project and prospers to a long-term Strategic Business Partnership, with PNV Lifesciences sharing similar risks and investing in building capabilities.
⦁ We continue to grow with same entrepreneurial spirit, culture of creativity and innovation and pride in belonging to an industry that makes a difference in the lives of people.
